Letter from the Secretary General

Dear Delegates,

It is with great pleasure and honor that I welcome you to the First ever edition of the Greenwood High National Junior Model United Nations! Over the course of 2 days, you, as delegates will be tasked with discussing the world’s most controversial and pressing problems- war, human rights, that never- ending debate on whether pineapple belongs on pizza, and a lot more.

It almost feels like yesterday, when I sat at my first conference. I was very nervous, and people around me who were far more experienced were talking about conventions and treaties I had not even heard of. 10 conferences later, I quote parts and even subparts of the same conventions I once never knew existed. Model UN is a very educational journey and a wonderful experience for anyone.

At first, you would feel like it is just teenagers in suits playing pretend in an extremely stuffy room, but once the debate begins there isn’t one person who would not enjoy it. Model UN is a great way to develop public speaking, critical thinking, negotiation, diplomacy and so many more that will come very handy later on. So future world leaders! Ditch the sweatpants and embrace the slightly too big blazers! An astounding experience awaits!

Yours Sincerely,

Yug Singh, Secretary General
